About

Daphnée
Levy

Director Assistant Director Script Supervisor Editor Director of Photography

Master's graduate in Directing & Screenwriting from ESIS (École Supérieure de l'Image et du Son, Paris), I have built my career across both sides of the camera — with a strong pull toward the set, the crew, and the invisible work that makes a film hold together.

"Having completed my Master 2 at ESIS and my work-study program at Lux Artists, I am now actively seeking new opportunities in film and audiovisual production."

Versatile and detail-oriented, I move between script supervision, assistant directing, and directing — on award-winning short films, Nikon Film Festival selections, and creative video content.
